Aerohive Stand Alone Tool

This is a Free APP build to help Aerohive partners and customers. This APP is designed to provide helps on site, to:

  • Troubleshoot connection issues:
    • Aerohive Devices to HiveManager 6 Online servers
    • Aerohive Devices to IDManager services
    • Aerohive Devices to HiveManager NG Online servers
    • Aerohive HiveManager 6 and HiveManager NG Onpremise to Aerohive License server
    • Aerohive HiveManager 6 to IDManager services
  • Easily deploy new access points by providing an easy way to configure:
    • Aerohive Devices' CAPWAP client
    • Aerohive Devices' IP Address
  • Quickly configure an Aerohive Access Point in Site Survey mode:
    • Broadcasted SSID
    • Radio parameters
    • IP address parameters
    • IPERF parameters
